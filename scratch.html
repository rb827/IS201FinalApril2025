About Me

This is who I am.

Hello! My name is Rebecca Soto. I currently live in Lufkin Texas and I am pursuing a career in photography while also attending Brigham Young University. I got married in March of 2023 after serving a mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints for 18 months in Paraguay. I fell in love with photography during my junior year of Highschool and I have wanted to make my career of it since then, whether that is taking photos for families and weddings or for a company like National Geographic. I have two dogs, Jasper and Lucy, that I love to play with and train. I am the oldest of 7 kids, the youngest is only 3 years old.
